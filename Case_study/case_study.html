About

Our school is the oldest business vocational school in Kansai area. We offer a wide variety of courses related to the business field. For international students, however, there are three courses available:

Business Administration Course

In this course, you will learn the skills neccessary to own a business or work as a manager in the future. Computer class and Japanese classes are taught simultaneously.

Accounting Course

In this course, you will learn the skills neccessary to own a business or work as a manager in the future. Computer class and Japanese classes are taught simultaneously.

Busisness IT Course

In this course, you will learn the skills neccessary to own a business or work as a manager in the future. Computer class and Japanese classes are taught simultaneously.

After graduation, students can choose one of these paths

1. Find a job in Japan

2. Find a job in home country

3. Transfer to 3rd year of Osaka Gakuin University
